Elysia combines cutting-edge AI, real-time computer vision, and seamless UX to create the most accessible sign language platform.

🤚

Real-Time Gesture Translation

MediaPipe AI detects 21 hand landmarks and translates sign language into text instantly with sub-100ms latency.

🧠

Custom AI Training

Record your own gestures, label them, and train a personal model that learns your unique signing style.

🔊

Natural Voice Output

Translated gestures are spoken aloud using AI voice synthesis, enabling fluid two-way conversations.

📹

Video Calls with Live Subtitles

Join video calls with real-time translation and auto-generated subtitles powered by WebRTC and Socket.IO.

🔒

Private & Offline-Ready

All AI runs in your browser. No video sent to the cloud. Your conversations stay completely private.

📖

Gesture Library

Explore a curated library of ASL gestures. Search, preview, and add them to your personal model.
